About Us

Gangotri Khadi is dedicated to promoting handmade khadi clothing and supporting rural artisans across India. Our mission is to preserve traditional handloom craftsmanship while encouraging sustainable fashion.

Our Products

  • Khadi Kurta
  • Khadi Saree
  • Handmade Shirts
  • Traditional Khadi Clothing

Our Mission

To empower rural artisans, preserve traditional handloom techniques, and promote eco-friendly clothing made with natural fabrics.
